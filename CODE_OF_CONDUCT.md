# Code of Conduct

ASR is committed to a professional, inclusive and research-focused environment. Members must communicate respectfully, critique ideas rather than people, disclose conflicts of interest and protect confidential information.

Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, retaliation, plagiarism, fabrication, falsification and deliberate misrepresentation are not accepted.

Report serious concerns privately to `ceo@asr-lab.online`. Reports will be reviewed with discretion and access limited to those required to respond.
