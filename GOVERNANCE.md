# Governance

## Decision levels

- **ASR Leadership** owns institutional strategy, legal identity, sensitive access and final escalation.
- **ASR Research Council** owns scientific publication decisions and research-quality standards.
- **ASR Website Administrators** own the public platform, deployments and operational reliability.
- **ASR Engineering** owns software quality, packages, CI/CD and technical security.
- Domain teams own day-to-day decisions within their documented remit.

## Decision process

Routine decisions are made by the responsible team. Material scientific, legal, security or reputational decisions require review by the relevant council and ASR Leadership. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed before review.

## Repository ownership

Every critical path should have a CODEOWNERS entry. Direct changes to protected branches are discouraged except for approved automation. Human changes should use pull requests with traceable review.

## Emergency authority

ASR Leadership may temporarily suspend access, automation or publication when security, legal compliance, scientific integrity or organizational continuity is at risk. The action and rationale must be documented privately.
